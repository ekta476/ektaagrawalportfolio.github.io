Meta Ads Campaigns

I have managed paid social campaigns for 2 real-world clients, setting up ad budgets, custom demographics, pixel tracking, and multiple ad creatives to achieve high-ROI results.

Case Study 01

Design, Editing & Digital Careers Academy (Academy Artistic Vision Bhilai)

Business Type: Educational academy offering professional courses in Design, Editing, Videography, and Digital Marketing.

Campaign Performance & Analytics Summary

We managed multiple localized Meta brand awareness and reach campaigns to drive visibility for the academy's courses. The campaigns achieved outstanding results, generating a combined total of 333K+ Views, 289K+ Viewers (Reach), and 17,347 Post Engagements on Meta platforms with an extremely cost-efficient ad budget.

Key Campaign Breakdown:
  • Ekta Web Awareness Campaign:
    Generated 125.8K Views, 95,145 Viewers, and 98,625 Reach with a budget of ₹197.62.
  • Durgesh Burger Awareness Campaign:
    Generated 69,146 Views, 65,283 Viewers, and 68,428 Reach with a budget of ₹196.45.
  • Shree Post Awareness Campaign:
    Generated 63,385 Views, 45,494 Viewers, and 47,202 Reach with a budget of ₹94.97.
  • Owais Thumbnail Awareness Campaign:
    Generated 54,443 Views, 53,134 Viewers, and 55,003 Reach with a budget of ₹141.48.
Audience & Demographics Target:

The campaigns resonated strongly with young adults aged 18-24, who accounted for over 70% of the total engagements (~55% Male and ~15% Female in this segment). The campaigns were run using an optimized Ad Set Bid strategy and tracked using a 7-Day Click attribution model to measure enrollment intent.

Case Study 02

Event Management Company (Book My Tyohar)

Business Type: Event planning, wedding decorations, birthday celebrations, and catering services.

Campaign Performance & Analytics Summary

We managed multiple localized Meta brand awareness and reach campaigns to drive visibility for Book My Tyohar's event management services. The campaigns achieved outstanding results, generating a combined total reach of 343K+ Meta accounts and over 340K+ impressions/views with an extremely cost-efficient ad budget of ₹771.62.

Key Campaign Breakdown:
  • Wedding Awareness Campaign (5th Sept):
    Generated 129,514 Reach with a budget of ₹299.59 (Avg. cost of ₹2.31 per 1,000 accounts reached).
  • South Indian Style Catering Campaign:
    Generated 128,771 Reach with a budget of ₹317.22 (Avg. cost of ₹2.46 per 1,000 accounts reached).
  • Anish Birthday Awareness Campaign:
    Generated 85,531 Reach with a budget of ₹154.80 (Avg. cost of ₹1.81 per 1,000 accounts reached).
Audience & Demographics Target:

The campaigns performed exceptionally well in the 18-24 age group (accounting for approx. 62% of total reach) and the 25-34 age group (approx. 27% of total reach). Instagram was the primary channel for audience acquisition, driving approximately 85% of the total reach compared to 15% from Facebook.
